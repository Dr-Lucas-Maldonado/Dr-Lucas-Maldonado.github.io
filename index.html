About Me

I am a Professor and Bioinformatics Scientist with over 15 years of experience applying Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) in medicine, biotechnology, agriculture, and biodiversity. My expertise includes transcriptomics, genomics, metagenomics, and pipeline development, delivering innovative, data-driven solutions.

I have led multidisciplinary teams, built high-performance computing (HPC) infrastructure, and published 19 high-impact articles, contributing to human health and biotechnological innovation.
